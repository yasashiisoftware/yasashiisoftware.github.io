Your Music Everyday

Everyday Music Player is a user-friendly music app that lets you quickly play the iTunes albums and playlists on your iPhone or iPad. The large controls and simplified, no-frills interface makes it easy to navigate your music. Whether in the kitchen, garden, work, school, or on the go, it just works! Also great for the non-techie friends or family in your life!
